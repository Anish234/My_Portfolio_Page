Portfolio:-

A full-stack developer portfolio should include the following:-

• Projects showcasing your full stack development skills.

• Links to your code repositories (GitHub, Bitbucket, etc.)

• Examples of your work with an explanation of your role in the project.

• A list of programming languages, frameworks, and tools you are proficient in.

• A blog or website to showcase your work.

• A portfolio of apps and websites you have built.

• Contact information and any additional resources.
